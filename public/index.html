About Me

I am a customer focused SAAS Product Manager turned Web Developer, who is passionate about complicated problem solving, lifelong learning, and supporting the people I work with. I have developed a passion and excitement for coding using Javascript libraries and frameworks on the back and front end. At the SAAS start-up I worked at before, I started off as an account manager and gained a strong appreciation for customer needs and clear communication. I was promoted to product manager where the product I owned saw an increase of daily users of 10%. My experience working with developers in this role inspired my desire to learn how to code myself and my goal is to contribute to a tech focused company where my skills in coding and creating awesome products can continue to grow.
